Talk: Alexander Van der Bellen

June 12, 2015

1 minute read

Alexander Van der Bellen, chairman of the parliamentary club and federal spokesperson of the Austrian Green Party from 1997 to 2008, gave an interesting reflection on his academic career as a professor of economics and gave insights into how he got dragged into politics to become one of the most popular Austrian politicians. Talk by Stefan Ruzowitzky

May 19, 2015

1 minute read

Last week, we had the pleasure to have Stefan Ruzowitzky here in Oxford. Mr Ruzowitzky is an Austrian film director and screenwriter who won the Oscar for Best Foreign Language Film in 2008 with his film ‘The Counterfeiters’ (“Die Fälscher”). In an interesting talk he gave insights into the film industry, his role as a director, and his perceptions when watching films produced by other directors.
